# Planned Game Features

This document outlines the features planned for the Oil Rig Simulator. These notes expand upon the high-level plan in `design_overview.md`.

## Base Game
- **Third-person exploration**: Players control a character who can move around the rig and interact with equipment.
- **Resource management**: Track fuel, drilling fluids, extracted oil, finances, and other consumables.
- **Asset acquisition and upgrades**: Purchase additional rigs, tools, and vehicles. Upgrade existing equipment to improve efficiency and safety.
- **Business expansion**: Acquire new leases, hire staff, and expand headquarters facilities to unlock more capabilities.
- **Equipment maintenance**: Repair and upgrade drills, pumps, and other machinery as they wear out or become obsolete.
- **Interactive jobs**:
  - Construction tasks including scaffolding and rig assembly.
  - Drilling operations with pipe handling and well monitoring.
  - Production operations such as managing pipelines and storage.
  - Crew management: schedule shifts, train workers, and handle morale.

## Story Mode
- A narrative-driven option that guides the player from building the first rig to operating a large offshore company.
- Missions introduce new mechanics and provide specific objectives.
- Characters and events offer challenges that tie into the core resource management gameplay.
